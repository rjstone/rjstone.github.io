If you own a YubiKey that wasn't provided to you by the IT department at your workplace, and you've had to figure it all out youself, then you've probably run into some problems. The documentation seems to be rather spotty and not really the greatest.

Here I will attempt to document everything I wish I had known to begin with, before I started tinkering with pretty much everything the device does.
